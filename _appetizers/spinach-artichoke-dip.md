<h3>Ingredients</h3>
<ul>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped onion</li>
  <li>1 stick butter (I put 3/4)</li>
  <li>2 packs frozen spinach</li>
  <li>1 can artichoke hearts, chopped</li>
  <li>1- 8 oz carton sour cream</li>
  <li>1- 8 oz cream cheese (reduced fat)</li>
  <li>2/3 cup parmesan cheese, grated</li>
  <li>1/2 cup Monterey jack cheese</li>
  <li>Garlic</li>
</ul>

<h3>Method</h3>
<p>Sautee onions, butter and garlic, add frozen spinach thatâ€™s been drained and cooked. Add artichoke hearts, sour cream, and cream cheese. Stir until creamy. Stir in parmesan cheese until blended. Put in a dish, add Monterey jack cheese on top, or mixed in with the rest of the ingredients. Serve warm with white corn tortilla chips or crackers.</p>
